What is Never Again?

Never Again is the ultimate to do App for any device¹ avaible on the web². You can easily save your tasks and acces them whenever you need since they are saved in your browser.


How does it work?

The webapp stores all of your tasks locally directly on your browser storage. When you log out and re-open the app you will find all of your tasks right there where you left them. You can also put your tasks in two different lists: one for common tasks, and the other for daily tasks



Where can I find it?

You can find right down here, just press the button and you will be redirected to the webapp.
